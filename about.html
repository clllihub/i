About

We craft sensory experiences to bridge digital-physical divide through captivating visuals, resonant sounds, and stirring moments.

  • Creative Thinking

    Considering, reconsidering, and exploring beyond the beginning and end of a creative idea. We are a design studio led by CGI expertise that pays particular attention to details.
    We specialize in creating vibrant graphic solutions with an immersive and engaging level of detail. This attention to detail enables us to create stunningly beautiful graphic solutions without compromising the principles of good, simple design.

  • Image Making

    We create fashion surreal, ethereal worlds with a contemporary aesthetic. Our work blends cutting-edge design with imaginative concepts, resulting in visually stunning and thought-provoking imagery. Each project is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring a unique and captivating visual experience. We strive to push the boundaries of creativity, setting new standards in the realm of image making.

  • Motion Making

    Our work is characterized by a focus on the interplay of movement, color, texture, and composition. We employ a workflow that nurtures our creativity and enables us to produce visually stunning solutions with freedom and authenticity.

  • Interaction &Innovation

    Our primary motivation, through the experiences and visuals we create, is to create an interaction.
    Our productions are born with the aim of enhancing creative ideas and reintegrating our senses into the thought process. Our work is to reconnect a creative idea with a sensory experience.

  • Meaningful Work

    For each project, we leverage our network of like-minded individuals to assemble the most suitable team to meet its requirements. In doing so, we can provide limitless creative solutions and ensure that our clients achieve optimal results for every need.

From our Parisian office, our lovely team crafts 3D goods, produces films, takes photographs, and designs campaigns. Crafting for brands, agencies, Institutions, and artists.
